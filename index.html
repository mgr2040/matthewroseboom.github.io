A little about me...

I am a graduate student researcher and teaching assistant in the Department of Geology and Geophysics at Texas A&M Univeristy. Currently pursuing a master's degree in geophysics with interests in fluid dynamics and heat transport, geomechanics, environmental geology and geohazards, and computational modeling.

Applications in Focus

A glimpse of my research and areas I'm interested in

Fluid Mechanics and Heat Transport

Flow in porous media, Subglacial and fracture flow in ice sheet dynamics. Steady and transient-state Darcy and non-laminar flow, Classical reservoir flow solutions

Geothermal Energy

Hydrothermal and Hot Dry-Rock Systems, Fracture deformation

Environmental Geology

Contaminated groundwater, Arsenic and other trace element contamination, Saltwater Intrusion, Pumping (drawdown and land subsidence), Surface-Groundwater Interactions

Geohazards

Volcanic flows, Lahars and debris flows, liquefaction.
